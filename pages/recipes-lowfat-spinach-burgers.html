Spinach burgers

50 calories per serving

This vegetarian spinach burger recipe can be frozen, letting you get ahead on busy days!

3,6g protein
5,8g carbs
1,2g fats
Learn more about macros

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 100g cottage cheese
  • 1/2 capsicum
  • 100g carrots
  • 230g onion
  • 500g frozen spinach

servings: 10

Steps

Using frypan, fry the onions for about 10 mins until pale and soft, then add the capsicum and the carrots (previously grated) for about 15 more minutes and leave it to cool a little.

Boil the froze spinach for about 3 minutes and add it to the vegetables. Add also the cottage cheese and mash together. Add the egg, until the mixture holds together. Divide into ten and shape into burgers.

Either chill until ready to cook, or freeze!
