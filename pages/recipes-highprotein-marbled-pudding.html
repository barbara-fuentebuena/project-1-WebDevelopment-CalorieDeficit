Marbled pudding

92 calories per serving

A simple pudding recipe with few ingredients comes together Marble pudding! Perfect for breakfast or snacks.

10,2g protein
5,7g carbs
9,3g fats
Learn more about macros

Ingredients

  • 10ml vanilla essence
  • 2 eggs
  • 35g vanilla whey protein
  • 35g whey protein - no flavour
  • 40g corconut flour
  • 50g natural sweetner
  • 810g cacao powder/li>
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 10g baking podwer

servings: 8

Steps

Heat the oven to 180°C

Mix all the ingredents leaving apart the cacao powder. Once the dough is done, divide it into 2 parts and add the cacao powder to one of them.

Spoon alternate spoonfuls of each mixture into the pudding basin until three quarters full. Using a flat knife, swirl the mixture to create marbling.

Pour enough boiling water into the tin to come one third of the way up the basin and steam for about 1½ hours or until firm.
