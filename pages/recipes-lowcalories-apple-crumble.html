Apple crumble

93 calories per serving

Apple Crumble is an absolute crowd pleaser! Very easy to make, this Apple Crumble recipe is a keeper that you;ll make again and again.

7,8g protein
9,6g carbs
2g fats
Learn more about macros

Ingredients

  • 20g cornflour
  • 3 eggs
  • 200g natural sweetener
  • 400g plain protein yoghurt
  • 4 apples

servings: 8

Steps

Pre-heat the oven to 190 C

Mix the cornflour with the sweetener and eggs to make a base in an oven container.

Placed the apples (previously peeled and chopped) all over the dough and take to the oven for 35-40 minutes.

Leave to cool and add yoghurt on the top!
